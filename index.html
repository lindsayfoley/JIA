Welcome to the Jesus in Action website

Every child has the right to a home and education, and our aim is to make a positive and lasting change in the lives of the children we work with.

By providing a safe and secure home, and by teaching them skills in a rural environment away from the city, we believe that we can help some of the most deprived children in Guyana.
